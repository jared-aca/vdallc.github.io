Services

Focused representation for the most common claim and appeal paths.

  • 1
    VA Disability Claims Initial claims through Board appeals and increased ratings; each structured around evidence and entitlement theory.
  • 2
    TDIU/Unemployability Total disability based on inability to maintain substantially gainful employment due to service-connected conditions.
  • 3
    DIC/Survivor's Benefits Dependency and Indemnity Compensation and related survivor benefits based on service-connected death.
  • 4
    Discharge Upgrades Representation before DRB/BCMR to seek characterization upgrades and correction of narrative reason.
  • 5
    Correcting Military Records Petitions to Boards for Correction of Military Records to amend errors or injustices in service records.
